About Olivia


Olivia produces chickens and eggs



Olivia is a chicken pasture located on West Seattle. Visit and have fun time with chickens and eggs. Connect with chickens, feel chickens! You will be able to touch, see, and smell real chickens. Check out Pasture open time for the best time to come. Also, participate in our Event! All events is a free and very-Olivia experience. Olivia hopes you will love Olivia.
